# Feastables Brand Styling

## Overview

To access Feastables' official brand identity and style resources, use this skill.

**Keywords**: feastables, chocolate, mrbeast, brand colors, typography, playful, high energy

## Brand Guidelines

### Tone and Personality
- **Tone**: Playful
- **Energy**: High
- **Target Audience**: Young adults and families interested in ethical and fun products.

### Colors

**Main Colors:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` - Clean white backgrounds
- Primary Text: `#000000` - High contrast black text

**Accent Colors:**
- Primary Blue: `#72E2FF` - Vibrant blue for primary calls to action
- Secondary Blue: `#00B3E3` - Darker blue for secondary actions
- Neon Yellow/Green: `#CEFC17` - Energetic secondary accent color
- Link Color: `#F2EBE0` - Warm off-white for specific links/accents

### Typography

- **Primary Font**: `Kanit` (sans-serif)
- **Headings**: `Kanit` (e.g. 36px for H2, 16px for H1)
- **Body Text**: `Kanit` (24px)

## UI Components and Styling Effects

- **Buttons**: 
  - *Primary*: Background `#72E2FF`, Text `#000000`, sharp square corners (`border-radius: 0px`).
  - *Secondary*: Background `#00B3E3`, Text `#000000`, rounded corners (`border-radius: 6px`).
- **Interactive Elements**: Elements highlight on hover to indicate interactivity.
- **Borders & Shadows**: Mostly flat design (no drop shadows on buttons by default).
